About Watch it Outside

Every winter, the Watch it Outside Film Festival becomes the ultimate gathering of original storytellers and audiences seeking new voices and fresh perspectives. Our annual program includes dramatic and documentary features and films; showcasing emerging media in the form of multimedia installations, performances, and films.
